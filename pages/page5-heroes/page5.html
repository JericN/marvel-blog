The HeroesThe Heroes

A hero, by definition, is someone who is admired for his or her courage, nobility and exemplary achievements. This person does not need to have superpowers to be a hero. Most real-life heroes are ordinary citizens who act courageously in difficult or dangerous situations in order to save someone from tragic outcomes. A hero can also be a teacher, a parent or an ordinary student.

Anyone can be a hero, anyone can save someone whenever a situation calls for it. it. But not everyone is brave enough to be one. The world needs more modern heroes, not those who are willing to sacrifice their life, but those who are willing to change the world for a better one.
